Joseph Stalin

Brief Overview

Joseph Stalin was born on December 21, 1879. He was a famous dictator of the USSR.
He industrialized the USSR and built up its atomic bomb program. He ruled the USSR,
with terror and he put to death anyone he didn't like or thought would overthrow him.
Some even said, "The closer you were to Stalin, the closer to death you were. He made
a pact with the Nazis called the Nazi-Soviet Act. This was a peace treaty, but in 1941 the
Germans invaded the USSR, but Stalin mobilized a defense and in the winter of 1942, the
Red Army had pushed the Germans out of the USSR.

Birthplace: Gori, Georgia
Deathplace: Moscow, Russia
